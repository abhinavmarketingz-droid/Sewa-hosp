# Product Context

## Vision
A **white-label, luxury hospitality platform** that any business can brand as its own. The platform must be fast, easy to configure, and modular so customers can enable only the features they need.

## Target Users
- Hospitality brands and concierge services
- Corporate travel management firms
- Luxury experience curators
- Destination management companies

## Core Product Pillars
1. **Custom Branding & Theming**
   - Per-tenant design tokens (colors, typography, spacing, imagery)
   - Theme extensions (packaged style overrides + UI variants)
   - Multi-theme preview and scheduled theme activation

2. **Page Builder & Content System**
   - Section-based page builder (hero, services, destinations, testimonials, offers)
   - Versioned content and safe rollbacks
   - Page templates and reusable blocks

3. **SEO & Marketing**
   - Structured metadata, sitemap generation, OG tags
   - Rich content schema support
   - Multi-language content support

4. **Commerce & Monetization**
   - Pluggable payment gateways (Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal)
   - Service packages and custom pricing
   - Coupon and upsell modules

5. **Admin & Operations**
   - Role-based access control
   - Audit logs
   - Exportable backups and media library

## Customization Requirements
- Everything configurable without code changes
- Extension marketplace concept (optional)
- Tenant-level feature flags and entitlements

## KPIs (Quality Targets)
- Time-to-first-render < 1s on fast networks
- Lighthouse Performance, Accessibility, Best Practices, SEO >= 99
- Editable content published within 5 seconds

## Dependencies & Constraints
- Must be deployable without dedicated DevOps
- Must support backup/restore by admin users
